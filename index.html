About Us

Face recognition for your business

Face API's services are versatile that meet requirements from low-volume users to enterprises.

We're building a Face Recognition platform that lets you quickly integrate human identity features into your products and services—it's speedy, safe, and secure.

Face API is a simple concept—you submit images into our API, and our deep learning algorithms analyze the faces found, then the API returns a bunch of useful data about the faces we find. You can use this to search, match and compare faces, or measure characteristics such as age and gender.

Face API Features

Face Detection
Detect human faces on your photo/image
Face Identification
Examine face matches. Question: “Who is this?”
Face Verification
Search for someone's face. Answer: “Is this you?”
Age Detection
Detect age groups: child, young-adult, adult, or elder
Gender Detection
Detect gender: female or male
Multi-face Detection
Detect individuals, crowds, audiences and groups
